This file documents how various modules are turned on/off in BSIM-CMG.

To turn off a module, set the parameters as follows:

Subthreshold slope degradation
	CDSC=0
	CDSCD=0
	CIT=0

Drain induced barrier lowering
	ETA0=0

Vt roll-off (at low drain bias)
	DVT0=0

Reverse short channel effect
	K1RSCE=0

Quantum effect
	QMFACTOR=0
	QMTCENIV=0
	QMTCENCV=0

Mobility degradation due to vertical field & Coulomb scattering
	UA=0
	EU=1 (to prevent 0 to the 0'th power)
	UD=0

Source drain resistance
	RGEOMOD=0
	RSHS=0
	RSHD=0
	RDSMOD=0
	RDSWMIN=0
	RDSW=0

Asymmetry Model
	ASYMMOD=0

Drain saturation voltage
	KSATIV=1000
	VSAT=1e9
    
Polysilicon Depletion
	NGATE=0

Channel Length Modulation
	PCLM=0

Effect of velocity saturation in the linear region
	THETASAT=0

Output conductance due to DIBL
	PDIBL1=0
	PDIBL2=0
        
Parasitic Capacitance
	CGEOMOD=0
	CFS=0
	CFD=0
	CGSO=0
	CGDO=0
	CSDESW=0
	CGBO=0
	ASEO=0
	ADEO=0

Impact Ionization
	IIMOD=0

Gate Current
	IGBMOD=0
	IGCMOD=0

GIDL Current
	GIDLMOD=0

NQS Gate Resistance
	NQSMOD=0
	XRCRG1=0

Flicker Noise
	NOIA=0
	NOIB=0
	NOIC=0

Thermal Noise
	NTNOI=0

Self-heating
	SHMOD=0

Gate electrode resistance
	RGATEMOD=0
